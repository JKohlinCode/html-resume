Hobbies and interests

Beyond my role as a system developer and GIS engineer, I find fulfillment in a range of interests that add depth to my professional profile. Programming and coding are not just career pursuits for me; they're hobbies that allow me to explore creative solutions and stay connected with evolving technologies.

In my downtime, I'm a gadget enthusiast, always eager to discover and appreciate the latest innovations. This interest not only keeps me informed but also sparks ideas for potential applications in my work.

Living in Västerås, Sweden, offers the added benefit of being surrounded by nature. I cherish the simple joy of spending time outdoors, often accompanied by my partner and our dog Nellie. Nature provides a much-needed balance, allowing me to recharge and approach my work with a clear perspective.

In summary, my hobbies serve as a harmonious counterpoint to my professional life, emphasizing a well-rounded approach to both personal and career development.
